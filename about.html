About Me

My name is Jenny Yu, and I am a biotech-professional-turned-data-scientist who loves exploring the Machine Learning and Deep Learning space! I earned a B.S. in Chemical Engineering and a M.S. in Bioengineering. It was during my graduate program when I started to really appreciate statistics and coding, and that experience has motivated me to keep learning more. I am excited about Machine Learning projects or applications that can potentially help people make better informed decisions. The purpose of this blog is to share my data science projects, and 'translate' key technical concepts to more intuitive understandings.
